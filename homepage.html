My Name is Christopher Guerrero

I am an actor & voice actor based in Dallas, Texas. After years as a stage performer, I transitioned into primarily voice over work in 2011. Since then, I've voiced in over 250+ titles.

A few of the companies I've provided voice work for include Crunchyroll, miHoYo, Bandai Namco Entertainment, Hi-Rez Studios, Netflix, Stunlock Studios, Okratron 5000, Gearbox Software, Capcom, etc.
